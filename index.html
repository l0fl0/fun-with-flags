Can you beat Sheldon Cooper at his own game?

How to play

  1. Guess the correct Country name to match the flag
  2. Answer Incorrectly and you will loose a life
    • Run out of lives and it's game over
  3. Correctly guess three flags consecutivly and gain an extra life
  4. Extra lives get added to final score
  5. Answer a maximum of 30 Questions
  6. Gamble your life!
    • An opportnity to double your lives or lose them all.
      • Decide before answering the next question

Are you ready?
